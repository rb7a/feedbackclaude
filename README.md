# Feedback with Claude

Feedback with Claude is an innovative web application that leverages the power of AI to provide instant feedback on social media posts or any form of textual content. It is designed to help users refine their messaging by simulating how different audiences might perceive their content.

## Features

- **Content Feedback**: Get immediate feedback on the tone and clarity of your posts.
- **Tone Adjustment**: Select different tones to see how your message could be perceived.
- **Audience Simulation**: Choose from a variety of audiences for targeted feedback.
- **Emotion Analysis**: Understand the emotional impact of your words.
- **Harshness Slider**: Control the level of critical feedback you receive.
- **Hashtag Recommendation**: Opt-in for hashtag suggestions to increase your post's reach.
